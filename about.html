Things that move are interesting!


I'm Tanay. Originally from New Delhi, I'm currently pursuing an MS in Robotics at Northwestern University. I recently graduated as a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering from BITS Pilani, India. As an undergraduate, I decided to undertake a number of different projects encompassing robot actuators, medical devices and assistive technology, hoping to find what I like. I was eventually drawn to robotics because of its interdisciplinary and practical nature. (Also, I want to be on the right side of the imminent robot revolution!). Since almost every aspect of robotics is so fascinating, I find myself on the path to becoming a jack of many trades, instead of narrowing down to one subfield. I've developed a strong interest in human-machine interaction, mechatronics, control, and perception.

In my free time, I like to go cycling, stargazing, play ultimate frisbee, cruise around on my skateboard, critique films, explore new music, and plenty of other things depending on who's around and what's happening.

I'm presently seeking relevant full-time positions. Feel free to contact me if you think I could be a good fit for your organization/project.
